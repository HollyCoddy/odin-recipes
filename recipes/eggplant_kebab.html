EggPlant Kebab

Description

This dish is prepared, especially in the summer when eggplant is in abundance. It is served as a main dish for lunch or supper. Usually pilaf and cacÄ±k are served together as side dishes

Ingredients

  • Boneless lamb shoulder or leg (500 gr)
  • Tomato (3 small size ~ 300 gr)
  • Eggplant (6 medium size ~ 1 kg)
  • Oil + absorbed during frying (6 tablespoon ~ 60 gr)
  • Onion (2 medium size ~ 150 gr)
  • Warm water (1 1/2 cup ~ 300 gr)
  • Salt (2 teaspoon ~ 12 gr)
  • Black Pepper (1 teaspoon ~ 2 gr)
  • Allspice (Allspice 1/4 teaspoon ~ 0.5 gr)

Steps

  1. Pare eggplants in strips
  2. Remove stems
  3. Cut into half
  4. Slice into 3 cm (11/2 inch) thick slices
  5. Brown lightly in oil both sides of slices
  6. Remove from to pan; drain
  7. Cut meat into 2 1/2 cm (1 inch) cubes
  8. Add to pan with finely chopped onions
  9. Saute over low heat until onions are tender
  10. Cover and braise for 40 minutes stirring occasionally; drain
  11. Add low warm water: Sprinkle with salt and pepper
  12. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes or until meat is tender
  13. Layer meat in a baking dish
  14. Place eggplant slices over meat
  15. Top with sliced tomatoes
  16. Add drippings
  17. Bake in a moderate oven for 20 minutes
  18. Serve hot
